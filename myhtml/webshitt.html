About Us

Our Mission

We specialize in creating professional entertainment programs that serve dual purposes - entertaining audiences while raising public awareness on important social issues.

Our team consists of experienced performers committed to preserving and promoting traditional Gujarati art forms while adapting them to contemporary storytelling methods.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of professional entertainment programs suitable for all occasions and events.

Professional Dramas

Thought-provoking theatrical performances that entertain while addressing social issues. Our dramas are professionally scripted and performed by trained actors.

Street Shows

Engaging public performances including traditional forms like Bhavai, Dayro, and Garba that bring communities together while spreading important messages.

Puppet Shows

Traditional and contemporary puppet performances that entertain while educating audiences of all ages about cultural heritage and social values.

Cultural Programs

Composite cultural performances showcasing the diversity of Gujarat's artistic traditions including dance, music, and theater.

Orchestra & Wedding Songs

Professional musical performances for weddings and celebrations, featuring traditional and contemporary songs to enhance your special occasions.

Garba & Dandiya Nights

Festive dance events featuring professional dancers and musicians to celebrate Navratri and other cultural events with authentic Gujarati spirit.
